# Privacy Policy for Twift

**Effective Date:** April 26, 2026

Twift ("we," "our," or "the Extension") is a developer tool designed to extract CSS design tokens for Tailwind CSS development. We value your privacy and are committed to transparency.

## 1. Information Collection and Use

Twift is a **local-first** browser extension.

- **No Personal Data:** We do not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information (PII), such as your name, email address, IP address, or location.
- **No Tracking:** We do not track your browsing history or use cookies to monitor your activity across the web.

## 2. Technical Data Handling

The Extension functions by reading the computed styles of the webpage currently active in your browser tab when explicitly triggered by the user.

- **Local Processing:** All design token extraction (colors, fonts, spacing) happens entirely within your local browser environment.
- **No External Transmission:** None of the data extracted from the webpages you visit is ever sent to our servers or any third-party services.

## 3. Permissions Justification

- **activeTab:** This permission is used solely to allow the Extension to inspect the CSS of the site you are currently viewing so that it can generate your Tailwind CSS theme.

## 4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

Since we do not collect any data, we do not sell, trade, or share any user information with third parties.

## 5. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by the "Effective Date" at the top of this page.
